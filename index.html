Seongsu Ha

I am a Master's student at Seoul National University Visual Information Processing Lab, advised by Prof. Joonseok Lee. Previously, I received Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research interest lies in the areas of computer vision and machine learning. Specifically, I am interested in improving the quality of multi-modal representations for various downstream applications, such as video scene boundary detection and visual grounding.
